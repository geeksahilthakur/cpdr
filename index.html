Empowering the Farmers of India and World with A.I.

The Crop Doctor app is designed to help farmers diagnose crop diseases and provide them with ways to prevent them. The app uses artificial intelligence to identify crop diseases and provide farmers with information on how to prevent them. The app also provides farmers with information on the best practices for growing crops. The app is available in both English and Hindi, and it is free to download. Crop Doctor is a must-have app for any farmer who wants to improve his or her yield and prevent crop diseases.

Click & Upload Photo

The Crop Doctor is an app designed to help farmers identify crop problems and pests. It uses machine learning to identify pests and diseases in photos that farmers upload. To use the app, farmers first need to take a photo of their crop. They then need to upload the photo to the app. The app will use machine learning to identify the crop in the photo and any problems it has.

Instant Diagnosis

Crop Doctor uses a machine learning (ML) model to analyze the infected crop by taking its picture via a cellphone. The ML model can be used to identify a wide variety of crop Deases, such as bacterial leaf blight, early blight, and powdery mildew. This is a significant development, as it can help farmers to save their crops from Deases.

Right Treatment Advice

The crop doctor app will allow the user to identify an infected crop and alsoo provide information about the best way to prevent the infestation of crop and to increase crop productivity. The app will use the phone's camera to take pictures of the crop and then analyze it to give a report.
